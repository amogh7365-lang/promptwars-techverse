COMPETITION FORMAT & ROUNDS

R1

Round 1: Prompt Accuracy

Focuses on prompt accuracy, instruction understanding, and constraint parsing under strict token and logic rules.

R2

Round 2: Creative & Innovation

Evaluates how creatively you use AI tools to build storytelling assets, graphic layouts, and digital branding copy.

R3

Round 3 & Grand Finale

Solve practical real-world AI problems. The top performers advance to compete live in front of expert judges.

rules

RULES & JUDGING

Competition Rules

  • Follow fair play principles throughout the competition rounds.
  • Only approved Generative AI tools may be used during the live event.
  • Plagiarism or unethical sharing of prompt patterns results in disqualification.
  • Respect fellow participants, judges, and organizers.
  • The decision of the jury panel is final.

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & Creativity: Novel approach to generative prompting.
  • Prompt Engineering Quality: Token efficiency and logic rules structure.
  • Output Accuracy: Fulfillment of context and formatting constraints.
  • Technical Understanding: Model parameter settings and control logic.
  • Presentation: Effective communication during the Grand Finale pitch.
faq

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do I need prior AI experience?

No, anyone interested in Generative AI can participate. We welcome all backgrounds!

Is coding mandatory?

No, some rounds focus purely on text prompts and logic reasoning. Coding is optional.

Will certificates be provided?

Yes, all eligible active participants will receive digital participation certificates.

Which AI tools are allowed?

A list of approved, public AI tools will be shared with registered participants prior to orientation.
